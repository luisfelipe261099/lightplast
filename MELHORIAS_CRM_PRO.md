### API Endpoints Profissional
```
GET  /api/customers         - Listar clientes

GET  /api/leads             - Listar leads

GET  /api/orders            - Listar pedidos
GET  /api/dashboard         - Stats em tempo real

GET  /api/export/customers  - Exportar CSV
GET  /api/export/orders     - Exportar CSV
```
